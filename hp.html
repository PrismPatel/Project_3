Himachal Pradesh

Introduction

Himachal means “snowy slopes” and Pradesh means “state.” Himachal reminds us of movie scenes and cozy vibes. It is termed as the "Land of the Gods" due to the stunning panoramic views of mountains covered with snow promising a holiday like never before. Vibrant yet peaceful life there is loaded with culture and heritage. Also the place offers endless opportunities for adventure activities.





It's home to scenic mountain towns and resorts such as Dalhousie. Host to the Dalai Lama, Himachal Pradesh has a strong Tibetan presence. This is reflected in its Buddhist temples and monasteries, as well as its vibrant Tibetan New Year celebrations. The region is also well known for its trekking, climbing and skiing areas.
